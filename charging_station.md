# Charging station

Project: a nation-wide network of car charging stations. Build a financial forcast for a typical charging station. 10 year, monthly. NPV/IRR, determine level of subsidies required.

Day1 is 1/1/21. Cashflows are on last day of month.

## Assumptions
* cost of land is $50k, paid on 12/31/20
* construction cost is $360k, and will take one year. Costs are paid monthly
* Charging equipment is $150k, payable 50% in adcanve on 6/30/21, the remainder on 12/31/21
* Contruction finishes on 12/31/21, operation begins on 1/1/22

## Debt
* 80% of investment financed by loan, pro-rata for every payment made
* loan repayments from Jan 22, over 9 years in equal payments (last payment 12/21/30)
* Interest 3% on A/365, from 1/31/21

## ECars
* There are 3 types of car: Minis have 25KWh capacity, Regular have 50, Premium have 100
* Assume 1000 cars serviced in Jan 22, with splits 10/50/40
* Growth rates in 5 years after operations start (22-26) are 2/3/1% growth per month
* Growth rates in following years (27-) are 0.5% per month

## Sales
* sale price witll be $0.35/kWh
* cost is $0.15/kWh
* Both costs and sale price will increase 3% per year from 2023
* assume cars will charge to 65/55/40% of capacity
